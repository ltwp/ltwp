Poetry Surf Club is a loose group of people who like surfing in the metro LA area.
Rules are simple. No injuries (broken bones or sunburn). Be chill. It's good to be out.
We surf just about every weekend, anywhere between Malibu and Newport Beach. If you have a friend in PSC, you're already in PSC. All skill levels welcome. Sign up for notifications via our newsletter, below.
